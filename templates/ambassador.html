Membership

Any youth age 16 up through the mid-twenties who choose to live by the principles and guidelines of the Seventh-day Adventist Church.

For the purposes of classification, the Ministry of Junior Youth describes the Junior Ministry as concentrating on those under the age of 16 years of age. The young people in the senior ranks are those aged between the ages of 16-30 and the ages of 22-30 and more split into two separate ministries. The primary emphasis of this club is on the elderly, although the cap on the age is left more open and in many fields young people in the 20s will greatly benefit from the benefits of membership in this club.
