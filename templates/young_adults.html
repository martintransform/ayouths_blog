Who Are The Young Adults ?

The Young Adults club is the Fourth club in the Adventist Youth Movement.

About 125 years ago a dusty country lane in Michigan began the tale of the Adventist Youth Society called Young Adults (YA) with two boys who kneeling in priesthood. This filthy street has now become a worldwide road network linking 10 million explorers in almost every political entity across the globe on more than ten million seven days. The story is full of the rainbow, bursts of happiness and high mountain experiences and deep tranquility and unsatisfied dreams. This is God's tale that leads a compelling military through the struggles of the major arching war, life as we now know it.

Mission/Aim/Motto/Pledge

AY MISSION

The salvation of youth through Jesus Christ. We understand youth ministry to be that work of the church that is conducted for, with, and by young people.

AY AIM

The Advent message to all the world in my generation.

AY MOTTO

The love of Christ compels me.

AY PLEDGE

Loving the Lord Jesus, I promise to take an active part in the youth ministry of the church, doing what I can to help others and to finish the work of the Gospel in all the world.

Bible Studies

No one has perfected the changes of lives more than Jesus Christ in history. How can we do it best, however? This website provides tools which will help learn His Word, the Bible. We do hope that the content on this website gives you an idea of Christ, his will to live and inspire you to study the Bible by yourself or share the experience with others.
